Winter WonderHack 2017

February 17th 10:00pm - February 19th 10:00am




Registration is open!

Join us for the COOLEST hackathon around! We encourage students from schools across the midwest to register. It's totally free! Late registrants (after Jan 31st) will not be guaranteed shirts or swag.

Go to Registration (Free)



Is this for me?

If you're curious and motivated, a hackathon is the place for you. Join hundreds of students from across the midwest to try new things, challenge your creativity, and "HACK" something together.

We define HACKING as using technology in a way that it was not originally intended.
Form a team ahead of time or join one when you get here. Learn how to program, construct robots, and build electronics and then use your knowledge to build a project. Workshops will be held during the event to teach you everything you might need to know. Work directly with our corporate sponsors to build relations in addition to your amazing project.
